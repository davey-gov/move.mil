--- layout: page title: Determine Your Moving Allowance ---

Enter your rank, dependancy status, and move type to determine your moving allowance. If additional questions arise, your Transportation Office (TO) can provide further information and generally is the best place to get help related to your move.

Note: This tool is based on the Joint Travel Regulation (JTR) which is the entitlements authority for all service members. Any differences between this tool and the Joint Travel Regulation (JTR) will be resolved by the JTR. Additionally, each branch of service may also have supplemental regulations to the JTR.

Your Moving Allowance

Moving allowances are generally based on the overall weight of your household goods. Any overages on your approved weight will be your financial responsibility, so work hard to make sure you stay within your weight allowance.
