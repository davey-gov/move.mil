Defense Personal Property System (DPS) is an online application where service members and DOD civilians can schedule their household goods move (also known as “self-counseling”) and submit any loss or damage claims directly to their Transportation Service Provider (TSP).

Use the tutorials below for help navigating DPS. If you get stuck or have any questions, you can visit your local transporation office or call the 24-hour SRC Help Desk at 1 (800) 462-2176.
