There are a number of services that the Department of Defense (DOD) and other government agencies provide to help make the military moving process easier to manage.

Military OneSource

Military OneSource has a bunch of articles about how to plan your move, find housing and employement in your new location, and how to deal with the stress caused from uprooting your family.

  • Plan your move guide
  • Moving with Children
  • On-Base Resources
  • Housing Information
  • Family Support Line

Military Installations

Everything you need to know about your new base including school districts, pet restrictions, and on-base contact infomation.
